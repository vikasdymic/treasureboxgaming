IF I DON’T LIKE THE APP, IS IT EASY TO UNINSTALL?

Yes, uninstalling is simple. If you don’t like TreasureBoxGaming, you can easily uninstall by following these simple steps:

FOR CHROME:

  1. Click the options icon in the upper right corner of your browser.
  2. Click on “More Tools”.
  3. Click on “Extensions”.
  4. Uncheck the Enabled box or click Trash icon to delete DearDestiny.
  5. Close your Chrome browser completely and reopen it. The extension should be removed from your Chrome browser.

FOR FIREFOX:

  1. Click the button with three horizontal lines located at the top right of your browser.
  2. Click Add-ons.
  3. Select the Extensions tab from the navigation options listed on the left.
  4. Find the extension you are looking to remove and click the Remove button.
  5. Close and restart your browser.

What if I have issues uninstalling? As mentioned above, we work very hard to make uninstalling as easy as possible. However, there are times when third party programs, such as security programs or uninstall utilities prevent this from happening. It is sad, but true that some security programs allow the installation of TreasureBoxGaming, but block our uninstaller. It is very annoying (to us and you) and when this occurs we work with these companies to unblock our application. However, in the interim, this may cause error messages or prevent the uninstaller from working normally. The easiest solution is to temporarily disable the blocking software or use Windows Safe Mode to allow the uninstall process to complete.

If you still have issues, please let us know – we are more than happy to help. Please share as much information including screenshots, error messages and any other specifics that will help us guide you to a solution. support@waysfinder.net
